bespoke-analytics

Slide 1

You just spent a good amount of time writing a nice presentation. But will people even look at it?

Slide 2

So you install bespoke-ga to get some statistics from Google Analytics. But will visitors reach the second slide? Or the tenth slide?

Slide 3

This is where bespoke-analytics comes in. First install bespoke-hash or bespoke-history. Then you add bespoke-analytics alongside bespoke-ga and to get per-slide statistics in Google Analytics.

Slide 4

When users switch slides, you should be able to see it as a separate page view in Google Analytics.

Slide 5

Note that the Do No Track (DNT) flag is respected by bespoke-analytics. But you are still more tracked than you think.

Slide 6

That's it, really. I was bored, and wanted to create a bespoke.js plugin. Enjoy!
